the ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00pm on Saturday, October 14th, 2017 in Redlands, California.

where? how do i get there?

smartphone operators can google 'three sisters farm'.
for the less technologically inclined:

  1. exit Interstate 10 at California Street
  2. head South and through the jog at Redlands Boulevard following California until it dead-ends at Barton Road
  3. turn Left and make a quick Right onto San Timoteo Canyon Road
  4. follow it for 9 miles. the farm will be on the left
the address is '30370 San Timoteo Canyon Rd, Redlands, CA 92373'.
